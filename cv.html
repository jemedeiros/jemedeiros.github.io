Short Summary


Studies of European Archaeology (specialized in Prehistory), Philosophy, Physical and Cultural Anthropology and Computer Science at the University of Freiburg (2008-2012, 2013-2019), University of Texas at Austin (2012-2013) and PUC University Rio de Janeiro (2005-2007).
