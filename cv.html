Short Summary


Studies of European Archaeology (specialized in Prehistory), Philosophy, Physical and Cultural Anthropology and Computer Science at the University of Freiburg (2008-2012, 2013-2019), University of Texas at Austin (2012-2013) and PUC University Rio de Janeiro (2005-2007). I am specially interested in the historico-genetic theory of culture.

Presently, I work at the Computer Center of the University of Freiburg. I am the Application Manager working at the Document Management System Project at the University, whereby the university is introducing a new DMS to coordinate and centralize digital processes of the university administration. In my Post-Doctoral Role I maintained ties as a Researcher to the Department of History of the Universidade Federal Fluminense . There I researched on the Daunian Stelae and ways to approach them from a cognitive perspective respecting the postulate of convergence of modern consciousness. Learn more about my research, lectures, seminars, and talks in the publications section of my website.
