About me

Studies of European Archaeology (specialized in Prehistory), Philosophy, Physical and Cultural Anthropology and Computer Science at the University of Freiburg (2008-2012, 2013-2019), University of Texas at Austin (2012-2013) and PUC University Rio de Janeiro (2005-2007). I am specially interested in the historico-genetic theory of culture.

Presently, I am a Post-Doc Researcher at the Department of History of the Universidade Federal Fluminense and work at the Computer Center of the University of Freiburg. Lectures, classes or talks on archaeology can be found under publications. You might also care to listen to the Podcast. I strive to maintain with some regularity. Under projects you will find an idea I had for a "monitor" of portable antiquities schemes in different countries. Unfortunately, I seldom find the time to continue and maintain this project. If you are my student, you are welcome to "steal" it from me. When I taught Portuguese language and culture at the University of Freiburg (ZfS and SLI) I started a Discord Server for any student looking for tandems and invited some native speakers ( join us ).

My hobbies include managing this website, surfskate, playing guitar (Jazz, Bossa Nova and MPB) as you can hear from the jingles, intros and outros on my Podcast and learning Japanese.

I love the sea.
