About me

European Archaeology, Philosophy, Physical and Cultural Anthropology, at the University of Freiburg (2008-2012, 2013-2019), University of Texas at Austin (2012-2013) and PUC University Rio de Janeiro (2005-2007). I am specially interested in the historico-genetic theory of culture.

I am presently teaching Portuguese I & II at the University of Freiburg (ZfS and SLI), where I teach lusophone language and culture, and I am also offering technical support in IT-Infrastructure to research and teaching at the Institute of Sociology of the same University.

An enthusiast of the use of technology in the field of archaeology, I am still learning with courses in Computer Sciences (B.Sc.) at the University of Freibrug.

My hobbies include managing this website, playing guitar (Jazz, Bossa Nova and MPB) and learning Japanese.
