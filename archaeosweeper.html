Archaeosweeper is an archaeological simulation. It works as the opposite of minesweeper: You get more money if you find the artifacts!

Just be aware! Every time you click you lose money. These are the costs of excavations or prospecting

But if you find something, you may refill your budget! Other times you will get points, but no money.
